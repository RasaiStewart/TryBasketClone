About Us

Welcome to TryBasket! We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated to helping you make smarter shopping decisions. Our platform allows users to track prices, create wishlists, and save big on their favorite products.

At TryBasket, we believe that shopping should be both easy and rewarding. Our mission is to provide the tools and insights you need to find the best deals and optimize your purchases.

Bask-it is a mobile app which will be used to combine multiple items from different websites to one checkout basket where one transaction is made.

The app will be made to reduce repetitive and time-consuming checkout processing’s. Time Consuming Multiple checkout processes due to switching between different websites to buy items. Websites encourage people to have an account with them, before having to add to basket to encourage subscriptions.
